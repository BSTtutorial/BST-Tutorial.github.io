Bible Readings

The bible tab at the top will bring you to the bible, with your daily reading pulled up. You can change your translation at the top left. There's also an audio version of the translations for you audio learners. There's questions and video's linked to every reading too, for your own use.

Chat

With the inbuilt chat, you can discuss anything. From random questions about the the Bible, to a hefty debate on the daily reading, there's something for everyone.  using the green plus button at the bottom right of the screen will allow you to post your own questions, comments, etc.

Prayer
